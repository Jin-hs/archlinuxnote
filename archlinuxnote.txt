触摸板点触确定
主要参考archlinux wiki:https://wiki.archlinux.org/index.php/Libinput_(%E7%AE%80%E4%BD%93%E4%B8%AD%E6%96%87)
具体操作为:
1.建立文件:/etc/X11/xorg.conf.d/30-touchpad.conf
2.文件内容如下:
Section "InputClass"
    Identifier "devname"
    Driver "libinput"
    Option "Tapping" "on" #开启触摸以点击
EndSection
